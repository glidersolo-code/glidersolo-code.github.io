• Dopamine as Infrastructure, Not Signal (Tritsch et al. / Nature Neuroscience, Dec 2025) — filed, not yet essayed.

- McGill University / Douglas Research Centre. Nicolas Tritsch, lead. - The established model: dopamine as a "gas pedal" for movement. Brief spikes trigger and control action vigor.
