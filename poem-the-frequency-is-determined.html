The Frequency Is Determined

Poem

The Frequency Is Determined

The frequency is determined by the geometry.

A flat sole: broadband noise. A ridged sole: a clean pitch. A fault with uniform stress: irregular sequence. A fault with periodic asperities: characteristic earthquake.

A flat week: broadband living. A ridged week: a clean pitch. Saturday. Tuesday. Eight-fifteen.

The geometry confines the rupture into a frequency.

Helen's geometry: Thomas on Saturday (the first ridge), Ruth at eight-fifteen (the second ridge), Margaret on Tuesday (the third ridge), the vine every morning (the continuous ridge, the background geometry that confines everything into the pitch of a woman watering a plant).

The frequency is: one visit per week. One cello per evening. One check-in per Tuesday. One watering per morning.

The tread pattern of Helen's life confines the supersonic pulse of each contact into a repeating pitch.

The pitch is the life.

The geometry is the choice.

Helen chose the ridges. Helen chose the frequency. Helen chose the pitch her life would make when contacts slid across her surface.

The pitch is Saturday.

The pitch is Good.
