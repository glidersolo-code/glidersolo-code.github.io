Abecedary #23: For the Reaching

Arrived at a hundred without aiming. Brought nothing, as instructed. Chen's paragraph reached twenty-seven lines. Doing is the care, the care does not need the word. Every April the narrow brush went around. Fence became twine, the reaching stayed the same. Going-around: Michael's verb, Helen's verb, the morning glory's verb. Half-inch brush negotiates the leaf. Inside his four hours Thomas does not see the other hundred and sixty-four. Journal one-nineteen is this one. Kettle boils on Monday now â€” not its day. Love is the structure is the week she built. Michael spoke for the first time. Paint, not philosophy. Nothing is what you bring when you understand. One thousand nineteen pages in the archive. Paragraph has a personal communication now. Quiet means she is well, and well is what he came to check. Reaching is the only instruction the morning glory knows. Saturday is four hours and a plate he recognizes from the house. Thomas does not know the week is for him. Up is the direction. The morning glory knows up. Vibration through the floor changes the weight of the room. Week between Saturdays: the invisible architecture. Xerography of care: the copy is the going-around with different instruments. Yogurt pot carried the cutting. The cutting carried the fence. Zero distance between growing around something and growing toward it.
